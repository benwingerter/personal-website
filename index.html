QuoteYa

Quoteya is an app where people can save their quotes and memories in the cloud. Users have a dashboard where they can search through all of their saved quotes. User's can attach images, geolocations, and dates to their quotes. Quotes can also be easily shared to social media. QuoteYa is available on IOS, Android and the web. I served primary developer of the backend server as well as the assistant front-end developer.

  • Client: Cairn, LLC.
  • Date: June 2016-Present

AirMeet

AirMeet was an innovative app that enabled easy interaction between attendees at large conferences. It allowed attendees to search through a list of other attendees, interact with a map that contained the locations of exibits and other users, message with other users, and view event-specific information. AirMeet was available on the App Store and the Google Play Store. The project was used by Creighton Prep's IT Innovation team to win the 2017 IT Innovaiton Cup. I served as the lead developer of the project. The project has since been discontinued.

  • Date: September 2016-April 2017
